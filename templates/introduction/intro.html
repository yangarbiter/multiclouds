What is MultiClouds

Easily integrate your sky-drives

    As Internet become much more popular, many cloud computing services come into existence, including sky-drive, wireless synchronization. Have you ever forgotten which sky-drive you put your files, taking much time to search all your sky-drives? Therefore, it turns out to be very important to integrate and organize your sky-drive into one system.
    MultiClouds is a free service that lets you do that. Easily integrate your sky-drives including Box, Dropbox, Google Drive, SkyDrive, and also greatly easy to use. Just need to follow the instruction below.


Step 1:
Choose which account you would like to use and sign in at home page.

Step 2:
After getting into serving page, you can add sky-drives

Step 3:
Once you choose add some sky-drive, it will need to input your account and password to connect sky-drive and MultiClouds.

Step 4:
After add your sky-drive, now you can download, delete, cut, copy and even share your files.
